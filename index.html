ABOUT

Founded in the Winter of 2009, Revolution is the Chinese Yoyo team at the University of Michigan. We aim to promote Chinese Yoyo as a performance art form as well as bring awareness to A/PIA culture.

PERFORMANCE

Revolution, in collaboration with Photonix glowsticking, hosts our own show each Spring: Yotonix.
Revolution has also performed at some of the biggest events on campus including CelebrASIA, Dem Viet Nam Show, Huaren Culture Show, Generation APA, Gayz Craze, TEDxUM, Lunar Ball, Festifall, Relay for Life, G-Men concerts, Dance Mix, and Dance Marathon.
We have also been invited to perform at campuses throughout the Midwest, including MSU, OSU, UToledo, UChicago, GVSU, and Northwestern.

CONTACT

Revolution holds open practices for University of Michigan students every week and welcomes everyone to come learn how to Chinese Yoyo!
